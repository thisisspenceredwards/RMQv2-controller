'CONNECTION_SUCCESS'

    is used to confirm connection to broker and controller

'CONNECTION_FAILURE' (Last will and testament)

    is used to send a critical failure as the controller program has likely crashed

'DATABASE_SUCCESS'

    is used to confirm successful database operation

'DATABASE_FAILURE'
   
    is used to alert that the database operation failed

'CONTROLLER_SUCCESS'

    is used to confirm successful controller operation

'CONTROLLER_FAILURE'

    is used to alert controller operation failed
